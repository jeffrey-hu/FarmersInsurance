## Minnesota Rules of Civil Procedure

**URL:** https://mncourts.gov/_media/migration/appellate/supreme-court/court-rules/MN-Rules-of-Civil-Procedure.pdf
**Source:** Minnesota Judicial Branch (mncourts.gov)
**Relevance:** Governs procedure in Minnesota state district court, where we intend to file. Key rules likely to be relevant include:
- Rule 3 (commencement of action / limitations)
- Rule 4 (summons and service of process)
- Rule 8 (pleading requirements)
- Rule 17.01 (real party in interest — supports naming FIE as defendant)
- Rule 56 (summary judgment)

# Claim Timeline — Lu v. Fire Insurance Exchange

**Case:** Yibiao Lu & Jie Hu v. Fire Insurance Exchange (d/b/a Farmers Insurance)
**Policy:** 98055-49-65 | **Claim:** 7007986837-1-1
**Property:** 17756 George Moran Dr, Eden Prairie, MN 55347
**Loss Date:** July 13, 2024

---

## Phase 1: Background & Prior Claims (2014–2023)

**10/22/2014 — Policy inception.** Yibiao Lu signs application and subscription agreement. Policy includes endorsements J6898 (Amending General Conditions) and J6944 (Limited Matching of Undamaged Property). Windstorm/Hail deductible set at 2% of Coverage A.
📄 `HomePolicy/2014_Application-PolicyDocument-980554965.pdf`, `HomePolicy/2014_newBusiness_policy.pdf`

**2015 — Prior hail claim inspection: no window dents observed.** This is significant because it establishes that no dents existed on any windows as of 2015.
⚠️ *No document in project — sourced from family's 2/10/2025 letter to Farmers. The 2015 claim file itself is not in the project folder.*

**09/05/2020 — Prior hail claim (claim #3013539499-1).** Roof and gutters replaced. Only 2 window sashes approved: F1.7 (1-sash window at corner of stairs, facing front) and B1.2 (3-sash window in family room, facing back). Due to COVID-19, repairs were extended but the 2 sashes and front door were never actually replaced before the extension expired.
⚠️ *The 2020 claim FinalDocument.pdf is referenced in several letters but is NOT in the project folder. This is a critical missing document.*

**After 09/13/2021 — Gutters and downspouts replaced** by contractor (evidenced by contractor text messages dated 9/13/2021). Any hail damage visible on these gutters necessarily postdates 2021.
📄 Referenced in `EventDocuments/2025-01-31-ResponseToFarmer-01-31-25-StormsAfter2020.pdf`
⚠️ *The actual contractor text messages are not in the project folder.*

**08/18/2023 — Renewal issued for policy year 10/22/2023–10/22/2024** (the policy in force on the loss date). Coverage A: $886,000; Windstorm/Hail deductible: $17,720.
📄 `HomePolicy/2023_Renewal.pdf`

## Phase 2: The Storm & Claim Filing (July–August 2024)

**07/13/2024 — LOSS DATE.** Hailstorm strikes Eden Prairie. NWS records confirm multiple rounds of severe thunderstorms across Minnesota with hail up to 4" in diameter; Eden Prairie ASOS station recorded a 48 MPH wind gust at 1:00 AM on July 14.
📄 `Evidence/Significant Wind and Hail in Minnesota July 13-14.pdf`

**08/09/2024 — Mary from Legacy (contractor) contacts the family.** Window leaking discovered.

**08/13/2024 — Jack from Legacy visits the property.** Finds dents on windows. Helps the family open the claim with Farmers.

**08/19/2024 — Family calls Farmers for status update.** Farmers confirms inspection will be conducted by Hancock Claims Consultants.

**08/20/2024 — Family calls Hancock** and provides Legacy's contact information to schedule the inspection.

**08/29/2024 — First inspection: Jayden Grimm of Hancock Claims Consultants** inspects the property.
⚠️ *No Hancock inspection report is in the project folder.*

---

## Phase 3: First Denial, Reopening & Second Inspection (September 2024)

**09/03/2024 — FIRST DENIAL.** Farmers calls and denies the claim by phone — before hiring any forensic engineer.
⚠️ *No written denial letter for the first denial is in the project folder (denial was by phone).*

**09/05/2024 — Family submits photos of hail damage to Farmers.** Farmers reopens the claim. Farmers adjuster Nic Brau calls to schedule a second inspection.

**09/06/2024 — Second inspection: Nic Brau (Farmers Claims Spec Rep Prop) inspects personally.** He and the family find dents on windows together; Nic measures window dimensions.
⚠️ *Farmers later claims "the file does not contain a report" from this inspection. Nic Brau refuses to provide his notes. This is a significant discovery/spoliation issue.*

**09/08/2024 & 09/10/2024 — Family submits additional photos** of hail damage evidence to Nic Brau.

**09/10/2024 — Nic Brau emails** that Farmers will hire another inspector, Nathan Prieve, for a third inspection.

**09/11/2024 — Family visits agent Peter Pietila.** Pete advises that damages need to be documented before the inspector arrives.

**09/16/2024 — Third inspection scheduled** for Nathan Prieve to visit on 10/9/2024.

---

## Phase 4: Prieve Inspection & Second Denial (October–November 2024)

**10/09/2024 — Third inspection: Nathan Prieve (Prieve Engineering LLC, File #241008) inspects.** Family hands him their damage documentation. Prieve later concludes: no hail damage to windows from any recent storm; all dents from 2020 or non-hail causes; no storm event documented on 7/13/2024; water leakage from wear/tear.
📄 Report included in `EventDocuments/FarmersDenyLetter-11-4.pdf` (pp. 4–10); photos in `EventDocuments/FarmersDenyLetter-11-4-AppendixA.pdf`

**11/04/2024 — SECOND AND FINAL DENIAL.** Farmers issues formal denial letter citing wear/tear, marring, corrosion/deterioration, inherent vice, neglect, and faulty construction exclusions, based on Prieve's report.
📄 `EventDocuments/FarmersDenyLetter-11-4.pdf`

**11/07/2024 — Agent Pete sends the family the Prieve report and photo appendix.**

**11/08/2024 — Family emails Nic Brau requesting a meeting** to discuss their questions about the report.

**11/11/2024 — Nic Brau refuses to meet.** Email states: "I have already responded with our finding/outcome in the letter/engineer report."

---

## Phase 5: Administrative Complaints & Appeals (November 2024–February 2025)

**11/19/2024 — Complaint filed with MN Department of Commerce** (Complaint ID: 93547). Includes rebuttal to Prieve's conclusions with stormersite.com hail data and photo evidence. Family works with Katherine Toomajian at Commerce.
📄 `EventDocuments/11-19-complaint-to-dept-of-commerce.pdf`

**01/31/2025 — Family meets with agent Pete.** Pete provides contact information for Bradlee Monson, field claim supervisor.

**01/31/2025 — Family sends formal appeal to Farmers** (to Bradlee and Nic), attaching a letter disputing Prieve's conclusions, evidence of post-2020 hail damage to replacement gutters, and stormersite.com weather data. Documents also uploaded to Farmers claim center.
📄 `EventDocuments/2025-01-31-ResponseToFarmer.pdf`, `EventDocuments/2025-01-31-ResponseToFarmer-01-31-25-StormsAfter2020.pdf`, `EventDocuments/2025-01-31-ResponseToFarmer-01-31-25-DamagesAfter2020.pdf`

**02/03/2025 — Family calls Bradlee Monson** to ask him to review the documents.

**02/03/2025 — Family meets with Katherine at Commerce.** Katherine suggests obtaining City of Eden Prairie permit records and canvassing neighbors.

**02/03/2025 — Family requests permit records** from City of Eden Prairie.

**02/04/2025 — City provides the permit list.**

**02/04–02/07/2025 — Neighbor canvass.** Family knocks on 40+ doors in cold weather; reaches 19 homeowners; **14 of 19 confirm hail damage** with insurance-covered repairs in 2023 and/or 2024.

**02/10/2025 — Family submits neighbor survey results to Farmers** with second response letter, including City permit data showing 25 permits for exterior work in the neighborhood.
📄 `EventDocuments/2025-02-10-SecondResponseToFinalReport.pdf`

**02/10/2025 — Nic Brau leaves voicemail:** Farmers will not review the new facts/evidence.

**02/12/2025 — Family forwards the 2/10 response to Katherine at Commerce.**

**02/13/2025 — Katherine forwards to her director** for additional review, but Commerce is unable to take further administrative action.

**02/14/2025–04/23/2025 — Family travels internationally.** Claim activity paused.

**06/08–06/09/2025 — Family contacts FOX9** by email and phone to request weather records.

**06/18/2025 — Family meets with John (volunteer lawyer) at Self Help Center.**

⚠️ *No AG office response is documented in the logs.*

**07/09/2025 — Family emails Pete** asking for feedback on the 5/28 submission.

**07/10/2025 — Family emails Nic Brau, Bradlee Monson, and Pete** asking about claim deadlines and requesting an extension if any apply.

## Phase 7: Appraisal Process (August–September 2025)

**08/03/2025 — Demand for Appraisal filed** under Minn. Stat. § 65A.01. Insured's appraiser: John Foster (retained through Pure Construction at $2,200, split with family). Sworn Proof of Loss states total claimed loss of $310,000. Three items in dispute: Windows, Siding (repaint), Siding (replace & remove).
📄 `EventDocuments/Appraisal Documents/AppraisalDemand.pdf`

**06/21–09/08/2025 — Email correspondence with Pure Construction** (Zach Sexe) regarding claim status, appraisal preparation, and specific window details (kitchen units W-247 through W-250).
📄 `EventDocuments/Appraisal Documents/CorrespondenceWithContractor(PureConstruction).pdf`

**09/12/2025 — Appraisal inspection and Award issued.** Results: Windows RCV ≈ $12,800 / ACV ≈ $6,400 — **hail confirmed as cause of loss.** Siding, paint, gutters: $0. Signed by Greg Tomes (Farmers' appraiser) and Wade Roos (umpire). John Foster (insured's appraiser) did NOT sign. Net payment: $0 (deductible of $17,720 exceeds the RCV).
📄 `EventDocuments/Appraisal Documents/Appraisal_Result_Front.png`, `EventDocuments/Appraisal Documents/Appraisal_Result_Back.png`

## Phase 8: Post-Appraisal Correspondence (September–October 2025)

**09/18/2025 — Family emails Farmers** (Zorphion Miller) asking which windows are covered and what F1.7 and B1.2 mean.

**09/18/2025 — Farmers responds:** Appraiser documented damage to "front right, and rear elevation in the dining room." Windows manufactured by Senco with stamps from 1993, 1997, 2008.

**09/19/2025 — Family requests appraiser's full document** showing positions of all damaged windows.

**09/19/2025 — Farmers responds:** Appraiser acknowledges "dining room" was a mistake in his report, but his corrected description conflicts with the executed Appraisal Award form.

**09/23/2025 — Family requests copies of approved settlement and prior claim inspection reports** related to window damages.

**09/24/2025 — Farmers responds:** Umpire did not provide a report or estimate. Refers family to their own appraiser.

**09/28/2025 — Family specifically requests:** (1) positions of the 2 damaged sashes from the 2020 claim, and (2) positions of damaged windows from previous owner's pre-2015 claim.

**10/01/2025 — Farmers provides a 2014 previous owner claim document** — not the 2020 claim records requested.

**10/07/2025 — Family requests Nic Brau's inspection report/notes** from the 9/6/2024 inspection.

**10/10/2025 — Farmers responds:** "The file does not contain a report on the requested date."

**10/13/2025 — Agent Pete emails Farmers** on behalf of the family, asking for descriptions of F1.7 and B1.2 window positions.

**10/14/2025 — Farmers repeats** the same "dining room" description from 9/18.

**10/21/2025 — Family emails Farmers:** Only 2 windows approved, but all windows are damaged as shown in submitted photos. "Why are they not covered? What do you want us to do?"

**10/21/2025 — Farmers responds:** "The claim was settled per the binding appraisal award received. The loss is under your deductible."

**10/28/2025 — Family emails Nic Brau directly** requesting his inspection notes from 9/6/2024.

**10/28/2025 — Family emails Farmers** (Twonda Miller, Zorphion Miller, Jed Jones) reporting active window leaks on a rainy day and asking again why most damaged windows are not covered.

**10/29/2025 — Nic Brau responds:** "All the information we can release has already been sent to you."
